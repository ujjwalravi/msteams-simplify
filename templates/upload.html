How we automatically assume the end time?

The downloaded MS Teams attendance file doesn't record the meeting end time. So, we have to somehow asssume the meeting end time. I'll explain this thing with few examples.

Suppose meeting was started at 01:01:15 and it is scheduled till 02:00:00. The script looks into alphabetically arranged names and selects the name in the middle of the list and sees it's joined time let's assume 01:05:16 and then picks the hr digit and increments it by 1. So, here meeting end time becomes 2:00:00.

Suppose meeting was started at 02:57:15 and it is scheduled till 04:00:00. Suppose the middle name joined at 02:59:05 and this script picked the hr digit and incremented it by 1 and hence sets meeting end time as 03:00:00. This will generate wrong results.

Chances of happening this is very rare but not inevitable. Still, I'll ask the users to manually write the meeting end time. This will generate you 100% accurate results.
